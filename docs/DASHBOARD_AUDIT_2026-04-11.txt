════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
THE DECISION FRAMEWORK
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

A swing trader needs exactly 4 things to make an entry/exit decision:

  [1] ENTRY   — Where do I buy? (entry zone, support, ATR, max pain)
  [2] TARGET  — Where do I sell? (T1, T2, R:R ratio, stop loss)
  [3] PROB    — How likely does this work? (prob_combined, IV rank, beat rate)
  [4] TIME    — How long do I hold? (days to earnings, OpEx, hold estimate)

Every field on the dashboard should serve one of these 4 variables.
A field that serves none of them is noise.

════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
CRITICAL GAP: prob_combined IS INVISIBLE IN THE UI
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

prob_combined is the most important probability metric. It is:
  - Computed in:    utils/prob_engine.py → compute_prob_combined()
  - Stored in:      ai_quant_cache.db → thesis_cache columns
  - Returned by:    GET /api/signals/ticker/{symbol}
  - In TypeScript:  TickerDetail.prob_combined (dashboard/frontend/src/lib/api.ts)
  - In copy-paste:  buildPrompt() in TickerPage.tsx (lines ~821-829)
  - Drives ranking: compute_priority_score() in ticker_selector.py
  - Sort key:       trade_selector_4w.py sort: ev_t1_pct → prob_combined → priority_score

BUT: prob_combined is NOT rendered anywhere in the TickerPage UI.
The trader sees "78% agree" (signal_agreement_score) as the only probability number.

Formula (PATH B fixed weights, clipped to [0.30, 0.90]):
  prob_combined = tech(0.30) + options(0.25) + fund(0.15) + catalyst(0.15) + news(0.10) + agree(0.05)

Components (also in API, also not shown in UI):
  prob_technical  — from rsi_14 normalised to [0,1]
  prob_options    — from heat_score / 100 with iv_rank inversion
  prob_catalyst   — from earnings beat rate (last 4 quarters)
  prob_news       — from Marketaux 7-day sentiment

data_quality badge: SHOWN in AI Thesis card header (HIGH/MEDIUM/LOW)
prob_combined number: NOT SHOWN — critical gap
